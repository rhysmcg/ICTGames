Quantum Odyssey

Quantum Odyssey is a captivating 2D platformer game set in a futuristic science-fiction world. Players embark on an epic journey as Alex Quantum, a brilliant young scientist who discovers a way to manipulate quantum physics, allowing him to traverse different dimensions. When an experiment goes awry, Alex finds himself trapped in a shattered multiverse, with each level representing a unique dimension.

Audio Maze

Little Pea Traverse

Little green pea traversing the world. The game is a platformer with many challenges. The character will encounter obstacles along the way such as spike traps, lava obstacles or just parkour itself. The unique gameplay to this game is that the camera will be chasing you throughout each level, meaning you will have a timer making sure that you make it fast enough

Immortality Knight

The main protagonist is a knight cursed with immortality for failing to guard the king. As punishment you are tasked with clearing the three spires of evil using your immortality.

Knight in Shining Armour

The main aim of the game is to collect fragments of an object in order to move on to the next level, which will require the player to avoid obstacles and travel across vast lands to do so.

Infinite Loop Guy

The game is of guy that got stuck into a infinite loop and cannot leave until he gets to the other side of all three levels. The first level is of a basic room for the player to get the feel of the game with a few obstacles.

I wanna go home

The game is a 2D platformer where a robot video game character will traverse through a simple land in order to get back into the portal to go back home. The game involves starting on a basic grass terrain, where it is easy to learn controls.
